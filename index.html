About / Contact

Hi, I'm Yenhsing.

I'm a licensed architect from Taiwan, currently based in Los Angeles. My background spans architecture, construction, BIM, and project management, with experience working on everything from commercial buildings to large-scale transit projects.

Lately, I've been spending a lot of time exploring the space between architecture and AI. I'm interested in generative design, computational workflows, and tools that help designers move faster from ideas to reality.

I enjoy working with people who like making things—whether that's buildings, products, systems, or new ideas. If that sounds like you, I'd love to connect!
